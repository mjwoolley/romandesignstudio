About

A studio rooted in simplicity.

We approach every project with curiosity and care, shaping environments that feel effortless and personal. From the first sketch to the final styling, our work is guided by proportion, material honesty, and the way people actually live.

Services

Thoughtful design for homes that evolve with you.

Roman Design Studio creates beautiful, functional homes through thoughtful design, styling, and curated selections. Whether refreshing a single room, preparing a home for sale, or planning a renovation, our goal is to create spaces that feel cohesive, timeless, and tailored to the way you live.

Design Services

  • Layout recommendations
  • Material and finish guidance
  • Paint and color consultation
  • Furniture and lighting recommendations
  • Project planning advice

Ideal for homeowners seeking expert input before beginning a project.
